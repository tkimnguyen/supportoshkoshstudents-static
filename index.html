Our Purpose

Support Oshkosh Students provides educational information about public education in the Oshkosh Area School District, focusing on school funding, facilities planning, and long‑term community impact.

Public schools are central to student success, workforce development, and the future health of the Oshkosh community.

About This Group

We are parents, educators, business owners, and community members who want residents to have access to clear, factual information about Oshkosh schools.

This site is intended to be informational and educational. When possible, it should reference official district sources for primary details.

Key Issues in Oshkosh Schools

Facilities and Infrastructure

Many district buildings are aging and require safety upgrades and modernization to meet current educational needs.

Funding and Costs

State funding limits combined with rising costs affect how school districts plan for maintenance and programs.

Long‑Term Planning

Long‑range planning aims to balance responsible use of public resources with student learning environments.
